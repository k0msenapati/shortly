Frequently Asked Questions

How long do shortened links remain active? ↓

By default, Shortly connection aliases remain active indefinitely. You can optionally set custom expiration dates during creation, and deactivate or reactivate links whenever you choose.

Can I search through my shortened links? ↓

Yes! Your dashboard is equipped with a search index that allows you to query your links by long URL destination in real-time.

Can I track direct clicks vs. QR code scans? ↓

Yes. We automatically track both total link clicks and unique QR code scans separately. You can view the full analytics breakdown on the statistics page for any link.
